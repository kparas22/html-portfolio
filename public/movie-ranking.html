The best movies according to me

My top 3 movies of all time

The Godfather

"I'm going to make him an offer he can't refuse".
This line from the classic crime drama is a quintessential representation of power and persuasion in film.

Casablanca

"Here's looking at you, kid" and "We'll always have Paris".
This beloved film has multiple iconic lines, with these two being among the most recognized.

Gone With Wind

: "Frankly, my dear, I don't give a damn".
This quote from the epic Civil War film was famously selected as the most memorable American movie quotation of all time by the American Film Institute (AFI).
